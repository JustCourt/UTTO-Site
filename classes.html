CLASSES

All of our classes are held at Hart House. For more information, visit their website.
Wear clothing that is easy to move around in, such as shorts and t-shirts. Wearing the club uniform is not required but recommended. Dobok is not required. If using the locker rooms at Hart House, please bring a valid T-card.

If you would like to withdraw from the class, to get a refund you mus withdraw before the second class. Withdraw will incur an administration fee.
